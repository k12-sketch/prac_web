Neapolitan Pizza Cooking Class

Join our exclusive, once-a-year pizza making class in the heart of Naples, Italy. Learn the art of crafting authentic Neapolitan pizza from master pizzaiolos.

Class Details

Our intensive one-day class covers everything from dough preparation to baking in a traditional wood-fired oven. You'll learn about ingredient selection, dough handling, sauce preparation, and baking techniques. No prior experience is necessary, just a passion for pizza!

Schedule

The class runs from 9 AM to 5 PM, including a lunch break where you'll enjoy the pizzas you've created. All materials and ingredients are provided.
