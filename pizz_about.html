About Our Neapolitan Pizza Class

At Pizza Elite, our mission is to share the authentic art of Neapolitan pizza making. Our yearly class in Naples offers a unique, immersive experience led by Chef Marco Rossi, a renowned pizzaiolo with over 20 years of experience. We believe in hands-on learning, using only the finest ingredients, and preserving the traditional techniques that make Neapolitan pizza a culinary treasure.

Meet Chef Marco Rossi

Chef Marco Rossi

Master Pizzaiolo

With over 20 years of experience, Chef Rossi is a leading expert in Neapolitan pizza. His passion for traditional methods and quality ingredients shines through in every class.

Our Philosophy

Our class is more than just a cooking lesson; it's a journey into the heart of Italian culinary tradition. We focus on the fundamentals: crafting the perfect dough, mastering the wood-fired oven, and selecting the freshest toppings. Our goal is to empower you to recreate the magic of Neapolitan pizza in your own kitchen, sharing this timeless craft with friends and family.

What Makes Us Unique

Our class stands out for its intimate setting, limited to a small group of participants to ensure personalized attention. Chef Rossi's hands-on approach, combined with our focus on authentic techniques and premium ingredients, provides an unparalleled learning experience. You'll not only learn to make pizza but also understand the history and culture behind this iconic dish.
